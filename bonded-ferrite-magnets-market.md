<p><p>Bonded ferrite magnets are produced using powdered ferrite materials combined with a polymer binder. The market primarily consists of two types: injected magnets and pressed magnets. Injected magnets involve the injection molding process, offering complex shapes and high precision, making them suitable for advanced applications. Pressed magnets are produced through compression of ferrite powder, resulting in a more cost-effective solution with simpler geometries. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, electronics, and renewable energy, each with specific performance requirements.</p></p>

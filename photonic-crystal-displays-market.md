<p><strong>The Photonic Crystal Displays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Three-dimensional photonic crystals</li><li>Two-dimensional photonic crystals</li><li>One-dimensional photonic crystal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photonic crystal display market is categorized into three main types based on dimensional structure. Three-dimensional photonic crystals offer enhanced light manipulation and are used in advanced display technologies. Two-dimensional photonic crystals, simpler in design, provide efficient control over light at the surface level and are popular in various applications, including LEDs. One-dimensional photonic crystals are notable for their reflective and transmissive properties, commonly utilized in filters and mirrors to enhance display quality. Each type caters to specific technological needs.</p></p>

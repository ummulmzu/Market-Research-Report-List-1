<p><p>Total Dissolved Solids (TDS) meters are classified into low-range and high-range categories based on their measurement capabilities. Low-range TDS meters typically measure concentrations from 0 to 999 mg/L, suitable for freshwater applications like aquariums and drinking water. In contrast, high-range TDS meters can detect levels exceeding 1,000 mg/L, making them ideal for industrial processes, wastewater management, and agricultural use. Both types are essential for monitoring water quality, ensuring safety, and maintaining optimal conditions in various environments.</p></p>

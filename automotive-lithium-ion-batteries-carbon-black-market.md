<p><ul><li>Lamp Black</li><li>Acetylene Black</li><li>Gas Black</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive lithium-ion batteries carbon black market comprises three main types: Lamp Black, Acetylene Black, and Gas Black. Lamp Black is produced from incomplete combustion of hydrocarbons, offering good conductive properties but lower purity. Acetylene Black is derived from the thermal decomposition of acetylene gas, providing superior electrical conductivity and purity, making it ideal for high-performance applications. Gas Black, produced from the combustion of natural gas, offers a balance of conductivity and cost-effectiveness. Each type plays a critical role in battery performance and efficiency.</p></p>

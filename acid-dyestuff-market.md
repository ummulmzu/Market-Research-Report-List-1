<p><strong>The Acid Dyestuff Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Strong Acid</li><li>Weak Acid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acid dyestuff market is primarily categorized into strong acid dyes and weak acid dyes. Strong acid dyes are highly soluble and exhibit excellent fastness properties, making them suitable for dyeing synthetic fibers like nylon and silk. In contrast, weak acid dyes are less soluble and typically used for dyeing wool, acrylics, and other cellulosic fibers. Both types serve distinct applications, with strong acid dyes favored for vibrant colors and durability, while weak acid dyes are appreciated for their softer hues and compatibility with natural fibers.</p></p>

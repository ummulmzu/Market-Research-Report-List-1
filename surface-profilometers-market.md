<p><p>The surface profilometers market comprises three main types: Optical Profilometers, Stylus Profilometers, and Handy Profilometers. Optical Profilometers utilize light to create high-resolution, non-contact surface measurements ideal for complex geometries. Stylus Profilometers employ a mechanical stylus that moves across the surface, offering precise, tactile measurements suitable for roughness analysis. Handy Profilometers are portable devices designed for on-site measurements, providing convenience and efficiency for quick assessments. Each type serves different applications in industries like manufacturing, aerospace, and materials science.</p></p>

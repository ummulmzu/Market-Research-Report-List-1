<p><strong>The Cranial Perforator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric Cranial Perforator</li><li>Manual Cranial Perforator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cranial perforator market consists of two primary types: electric cranial perforators and manual cranial perforators. Electric cranial perforators utilize motorized mechanisms to create precise holes in the skull, improving operational efficiency and reducing manual effort. In contrast, manual cranial perforators rely on hand-operated tools, offering surgeons control and tactile feedback during procedures. Both types are essential in neurosurgical applications, with their adoption influenced by factors such as procedure complexity, surgeon preference, and the need for accuracy and speed in cranial surgeries.</p></p>

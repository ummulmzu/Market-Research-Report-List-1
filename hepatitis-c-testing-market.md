<p><strong>The Hepatitis C Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Virus</li><li>For Antibodies</li><li>For Antigens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hepatitis C testing market is categorized into three main types: virus testing, antibody testing, and antigen testing. Virus testing detects the presence of the Hepatitis C virus (HCV) itself, confirming an active infection. Antibody testing identifies antibodies produced in response to HCV, indicating past or present infection. Antigen testing focuses on detecting HCV proteins, often used in conjunction with antibody tests for early diagnosis. Together, these testing methods facilitate effective screening, diagnosis, and management of Hepatitis C.</p></p>

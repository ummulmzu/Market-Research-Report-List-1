Colloids, including dextran and hydroxyethyl starch, provide volume expansion and are used in cases of shock or surgery. Other solutions encompass blood products and lipid emulsions, catering to diverse patient needs. This market caters to clinical settings, emphasizing hydration, nutrient delivery, and therapeutic interventions.</p></p>

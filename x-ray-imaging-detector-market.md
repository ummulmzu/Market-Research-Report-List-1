<p><strong>The X-ray Imaging Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Detector</li><li>Indirect Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The X-ray imaging detector market is primarily divided into two types: direct detectors and indirect detectors. Direct detectors convert X-ray photons directly into electrical signals, offering high spatial resolution and faster imaging. They are often used in applications requiring precise imaging, such as in cancer detection. Indirect detectors, on the other hand, use a scintillator to convert X-rays into visible light, which is then detected by a sensor. These are typically more cost-effective and widely adopted in radiography and fluoroscopy applications.</p></p>

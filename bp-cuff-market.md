<p><ul><li>Single-airbag BP Cuff</li><li>Multi-airbag BP Cuff</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The BP cuff market is categorized into single-airbag and multi-airbag types based on their design and functionality. Single-airbag BP cuffs use one air chamber to measure blood pressure, offering simplicity and ease of use, typically in home settings. In contrast, multi-airbag BP cuffs feature several air chambers, providing more accurate readings by adjusting pressure uniformly around the arm. This type is often preferred in clinical environments where precision is critical. Both types cater to different user needs and preferences.</p></p>

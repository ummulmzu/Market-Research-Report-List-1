<p><p>The pneumatically actuated ball valves market is segmented by connection types, including threaded, flanged, butt weld, and socket weld connections. Threaded connections are commonly used for smaller pipes, ensuring ease of installation. Flanged connections provide robust support for larger pipelines, allowing for easy disassembly. Butt weld connections create a seamless join, enhancing flow characteristics and pressure retention. Socket weld connections offer a compact design for high-pressure applications, ensuring strength and reliability in various industrial settings.</p></p>

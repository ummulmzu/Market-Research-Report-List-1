<p><strong>The Aluminum-plastic Composite Pipe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PE/AL/PE</li><li>PE/AL/XPE</li><li>XPE/AL/XPE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum-plastic composite pipe market features distinct types designed for various applications. The PE/AL/PE type consists of a polyethylene outer layer, an aluminum core for strength, and another polyethylene layer for protection. The PE/AL/XPE variant includes cross-linked polyethylene, enhancing thermal and pressure resistance. The XPE/AL/XPE type utilizes cross-linked polyethylene on both sides of the aluminum layer, offering superior flexibility and durability. These configurations cater to diverse needs in plumbing, heating, and industrial sectors, emphasizing efficiency and reliability.</p></p>

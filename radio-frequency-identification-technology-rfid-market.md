<p><p>Radio Frequency Identification Technology (RFID) utilizes electromagnetic fields to automatically identify and track tags attached to objects. The market is categorized into three types: Passive RFID, which relies on an external reader for power and has a limited range; Semi-active RFID, which has a battery to power the chip but still requires a reader; and Active RFID, which features its own power source, allowing for longer-range communication and more advanced functionalities. Each type serves various application needs in industries like logistics and inventory management.</p></p>

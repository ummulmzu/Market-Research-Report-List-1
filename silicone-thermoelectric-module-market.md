<p><p>The silicone thermoelectric module market is divided into two primary types: single-level modules and multi-level modules. Single-level modules feature a straightforward design with a single thermoelectric layer, making them simpler and typically less expensive to manufacture. In contrast, multi-level modules incorporate multiple thermoelectric layers, enhancing their efficiency and power output. This layered approach allows for better heat management and greater operational versatility, catering to applications requiring more significant thermoelectric performance, such as automotive or industrial uses.</p></p>

<p><p>DNA analysis in the government sector encompasses various market types, including Restriction Fragment Length Polymorphism (RFLP), Short Tandem Repeat (STR) Analysis, and Single Nucleotide Polymorphism (SNP) Analysis. RFLP is used in forensic science for genetic fingerprinting and paternity testing. STR analysis focuses on specific repeating sequences in DNA, commonly employed in criminal investigations. SNP analysis detects variations in the DNA sequence and aids in population studies and personalized medicine. Other methods may include mitochondrial DNA analysis and whole-genome sequencing for diverse applications.</p></p>

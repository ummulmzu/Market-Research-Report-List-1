<p><p>Materials Management Information Systems (MMIS) are categorized into two main market types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise MMIS involves software installed and maintained locally on a company’s servers, offering greater control over data and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing IT support. In contrast, cloud-based MMIS solutions are hosted on remote servers, allowing for easy access, scalability, and lower initial costs, while also reducing the burden of maintenance and upgrades for organizations.</p></p>

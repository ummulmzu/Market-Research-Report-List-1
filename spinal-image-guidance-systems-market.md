<p><p>Spinal Image Guidance Systems encompass various imaging modalities essential for precise spinal interventions. X-ray provides real-time visualization for accurate needle placement. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) offers detailed soft tissue contrast, aiding in the assessment of spinal conditions. Computed Tomography (CT) supplies cross-sectional images that enhance visualization of bony structures. The 3D Fluoroscopy Technique integrates real-time imaging with advanced software, enabling dynamic visualization during procedures. Together, these technologies improve accuracy, minimize complications, and enhance patient outcomes in spinal surgeries.</p></p>

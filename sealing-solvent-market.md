<p><p>The sealing solvent market encompasses three primary types: Uncured Type, Fast Curing Type, and Slow Curing Type. Uncured types remain workable for extended periods, allowing for adjustments before setting. Fast curing types are designed to set rapidly, ideal for quick repairs and applications where time is critical. Conversely, slow curing types allow for more thorough application processes, often providing better adhesion and durability. Each type serves specific needs based on application requirements and drying times.</p></p>

<p><p>Electric hydrofoil surfboards feature two main hull types: carbon fiber and drop stitch. In contrast, drop stitch hulls are constructed with a unique stitching technique, resulting in an inflatable, flexible design that offers greater stability and buoyancy, appealing to beginners or casual riders. Both types cater to varying preferences and skill levels within the growing hydrofoil market.</p></p>

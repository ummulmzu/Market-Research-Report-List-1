<p><p>The procedural stretchers market includes three primary types: Fixed Stretchers, Adjustable Stretchers, and Stretcher Chairs. Adjustable Stretchers allow height and angle adjustments, enhancing accessibility and comfort during procedures. Stretcher Chairs provide versatility, transforming from a seated to a horizontal position, facilitating patient transport in confined spaces.

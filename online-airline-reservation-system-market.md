<p><p>The Online Airline Reservation System market can be categorized into two primary types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based systems are hosted on remote servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and ease of access from any internet-enabled device. They typically require lower upfront investments and provide automatic updates. In contrast, on-premises systems are installed locally on airline servers, providing greater control and customization but often demanding higher initial costs and ongoing maintenance. Each type caters to varying organizational needs and resource availability.</p></p>

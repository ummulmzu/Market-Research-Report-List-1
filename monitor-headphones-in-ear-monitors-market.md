<p><ul><li>Dynamic Headphones</li><li>Moving Iron Headphones</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Monitor Headphones market primarily features two types: Dynamic Headphones and Moving Iron Headphones. Dynamic Headphones utilize a diaphragm powered by an electromagnetic coil, delivering rich bass and a wide soundstage, making them popular for general audio use. In contrast, Moving Iron Headphones use a miniature driver that offers precise sound reproduction and is often preferred by professionals for critical listening applications. Both types cater to different preferences, with dynamic headphones favored for their warmth and moving iron models valued for their accuracy.</p></p>

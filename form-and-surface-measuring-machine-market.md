<p><p>The Form and Surface Measuring Machine market is divided into two primary types: contact and non-contact measuring machines. Contact type machines utilize physical probes to directly touch the surface of the object, providing precise dimensional and surface quality measurements. In contrast, non-contact type machines employ optical or laser technologies to assess surfaces without direct interaction, allowing for high-speed measurements and the ability to evaluate delicate or soft materials. Both types serve various applications in manufacturing and quality control.</p></p>

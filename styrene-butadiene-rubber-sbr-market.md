<p><p>Styrene-butadiene rubber (SBR) is a synthetic rubber commonly used in tire manufacturing and various industrial applications. The market for SBR can be categorized into two main types: emulsion type and solution type. Emulsion-type SBR is produced through aqueous polymerization, resulting in better processing characteristics and improved wet traction, making it ideal for tires. In contrast, solution-type SBR is manufactured using organic solvents, offering enhanced strength and abrasion resistance, commonly utilized in high-performance applications. Both types cater to diverse industry demands.</p></p>

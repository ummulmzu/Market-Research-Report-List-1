<p><p>Condensate tanks are categorized into vertical and horizontal types, with each serving specific applications in the storage of condensate. Vertical tanks are designed for limited ground space, featuring a smaller footprint and higher storage capacity, making them ideal for tight installations. In contrast, horizontal tanks are often preferred for their ease of access and maintenance; they provide a larger surface area for thermal exchange. Both types are essential in industrial processes, influencing efficiency and operational safety in condensate management.</p></p>

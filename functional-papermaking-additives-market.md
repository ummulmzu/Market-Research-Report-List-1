<p><p>The functional papermaking additives market encompasses various types, primarily including dry strength additives, wet strength additives, and sizing agents. Dry strength additives enhance the tensile properties and durability of paper during production, while wet strength additives improve the paper's integrity when exposed to moisture. Sizing agents, on the other hand, control the absorbency and ink receptivity of paper, ensuring a smooth printing surface. These additives play crucial roles in optimizing paper quality and performance in various applications.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Microtunnel Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Earth Pressure Balance Machines</li><li>Slurry Shield (SS)</li><li>Pilot Tube Microtunneling (PTMT)</li><li>Auger Boring</li><li>Vacuum Extraction TBM</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microtunnel machine market comprises various types designed for efficient underground construction. Earth Pressure Balance Machines (EPBMs) utilize soil pressure to stabilize the tunnel face, while Slurry Shields (SS) employ a viscous fluid for ground support. Pilot Tube Microtunneling (PTMT) uses a pilot tube to create a path before installing the main pipes. Auger Boring employs a rotating screw to excavate soil. Vacuum Extraction TBMs utilize suction to remove material. Other specialized machines cater to unique tunneling needs.</p></p>

<p><p>The autotransformer market is primarily divided into two types: single-phase and three-phase. In contrast, three-phase autotransformers are designed for larger industrial applications, efficiently managing higher power loads and offering better performance in voltage regulation. Both types serve specific requirements, with single-phase models focused on lower power needs, while three-phase models cater to heavy-duty electrical systems.</p></p>

<p><p>EEG Brain Imaging, or Electroencephalography, is a non-invasive technique used to record electrical activity of the brain. Moreover, the growing demand for portable EEG devices and telemedicine solutions is reshaping market dynamics. Trends such as the use of EEG in mental health assessments and brain-computer interface innovations further underscore the market's potential. As research continues to unveil the brain's complexities, EEG technology is becoming integral to both clinical applications and neuroscience research.</p></p>

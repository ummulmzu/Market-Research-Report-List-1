<p><strong>The Radiation Survey Meters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Scintillation Detector</li><li>Nuetron Detector</li><li>Geiger Counter</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiation survey meters encompass various types designed for specific detection purposes. Scintillation detectors utilize crystals to measure gamma and beta radiation, providing high sensitivity. Neutron detectors identify neutron radiation, essential for nuclear applications. Geiger counters, known for their portability, detect ionizing radiation and are commonly used in safety monitoring. Additionally, the "Other" market segment includes specialized devices like dosimeters and ionization chambers, catering to unique requirements in research, medical, and industrial sectors, enhancing overall radiation safety.</p></p>

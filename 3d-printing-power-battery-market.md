<p><p>The 3D printing power battery market features two primary types: solid-state lithium batteries and solid-state sodium-ion batteries. Solid-state lithium batteries utilize lithium ions in a solid electrolyte, offering higher energy density, improved safety, and longer life spans. In contrast, solid-state sodium-ion batteries leverage abundant sodium resources, presenting a cost-effective alternative with good performance. Both technologies benefit from 3D printing methods, allowing for customized design, enhanced production efficiency, and the potential for complex geometries in battery construction.</p></p>

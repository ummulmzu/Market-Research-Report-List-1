<p><p>The TFT-LCD and OLED masks market encompasses various types of photomasks essential for display manufacturing. TFT-LCD photomasks are used in the fabrication of thin-film transistor liquid crystal displays, enabling precise patterning on glass substrates. Fine Metal Masks (FMM) serve OLED production by allowing selective deposition of organic materials for improved display performance. Open masks for OLED facilitate the direct application of materials, minimizing wastage. Additionally, other mask types may include specialized or hybrid solutions catering to specific manufacturing needs in display technologies.</p></p>

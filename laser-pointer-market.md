<p><p>The laser pointer market is categorized into Class 2 and Class 3R products, based on output power and safety regulations. Class 2 laser pointers emit low-level laser light (up to 1 milliwatt) and are considered safe for brief exposure, commonly used in presentations and educational settings. In contrast, Class 3R products produce higher power (up to 5 milliwatts), requiring more caution due to potential eye hazards. These pointers are often utilized in professional environments, such as design and specialized training.</p></p>

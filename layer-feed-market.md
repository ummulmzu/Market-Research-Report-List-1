Concentrated feed, typically rich in protein and energy, is formulated to be supplemented with other ingredients to create a complete diet. Premixed feed contains a blend of vitamins and minerals, designed to be added to a base feed to meet specific nutritional requirements. Each type serves unique roles in poultry nutrition.</p></p>

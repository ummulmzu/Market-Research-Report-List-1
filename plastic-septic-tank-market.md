<p><p>The plastic septic tank market mainly comprises three types: polyethylene, copolymer polypropylene, and fiberglass. Polyethylene tanks are lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and affordable, making them popular for residential use. Copolymer polypropylene tanks offer enhanced durability and chemical resistance, ideal for demanding environments. Fiberglass tanks are renowned for their strength and longevity but are typically more expensive.

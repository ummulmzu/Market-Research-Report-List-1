<p><p>Team collaboration software can be categorized into two main market types: the on-premises model and the Software as a Service (SaaS) model. The on-premises model requires organizations to install and maintain the software on their own servers, offering greater control and customization but demanding significant upfront investment and IT resources. In contrast, the SaaS model provides cloud-based solutions where software is hosted by a provider, enabling easy access, scalability, and lower initial costs while typically requiring ongoing subscription fees.</p></p>

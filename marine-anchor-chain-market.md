<p><strong>The Marine Anchor Chain Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>StudLink</li><li>StudlessLink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine anchor chain market consists of two primary types: studlink and studless link chains. Studlink chains feature regularly spaced studs, enhancing strength and preventing kinking, making them ideal for deep-sea applications. Studless link chains, on the other hand, are characterized by their solid links without studs, offering smoother operation and easier handling, suitable for shallower waters. Both types are essential in anchoring vessels, with distinct advantages depending on specific maritime requirements and environmental conditions.</p></p>

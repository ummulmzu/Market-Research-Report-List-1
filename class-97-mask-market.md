<p><strong>The Class 97 Mask Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>N97</li><li>P97</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Class 97 masks are categorized into two market types: N97 and P97. The N97 mask is designed to filter at least 97% of airborne particles, providing protection against non-oily aerosols, making it suitable for various environments, including healthcare. The P97 mask, on the other hand, offers a similar filtration efficiency but is specifically designed to handle oily aerosols. This differentiation allows users to select the appropriate mask type based on their specific exposure risks and environmental conditions.</p></p>

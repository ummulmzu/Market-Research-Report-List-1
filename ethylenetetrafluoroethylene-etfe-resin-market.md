<p><p>Ethylenetetrafluoroethylene (ETFE) resin is available in two primary market types: granular and powder. Granular ETFE is characterized by its larger particle size, making it suitable for applications requiring enhanced flow properties and easier handling, often used in extrusion and injection molding processes. On the other hand, powder ETFE presents a finer particle size, ideal for coatings and specialized molding techniques. Both forms leverage ETFE's excellent chemical resistance, thermal stability, and mechanical properties, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
